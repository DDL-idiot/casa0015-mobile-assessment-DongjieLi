Our Motivation and Vision

Motivation & Vision

In the digital age, everyone is surrounded by countless subscription services (actively or accidentally).Save time&cost, Optimize consumption culture, Aware offer&deal, Reinforce community connections, we want you SOAR in the digital sky.

We want to be a bridge connecting users, service providers and community in the digital lifestyle.Redefine subscription management with ‘Smart Subscriptions’, allowing users to easily manage, share and optimize all their services.

Painpoints

Centrally manage all subscriptions, whether online or offline. Clear cost statistics and renewal reminders to avoid overdue fees. The built-in news section provides discounts and new information on the latest subscription services.
