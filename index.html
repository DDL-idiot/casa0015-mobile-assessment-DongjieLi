Redefine Subscription Management

“Smart Subscriptions” is a comprehensive platform designed to help users efficiently track and manage all their online and offline subscription services. Besides, the built-in news section provides discounts and new information on the latest services.

  • Learn More

App Features

  • Alarm & Statistics

    Subscription tracking, cost statistics, renewal reminders.

  • Advice & Analyses

    Provide personalized recommendations and subscription analysis based on user behavior.

  • Alerts & Awareness

    Instantly notify users about subscription updates, offers and alerts to increase user awareness of subscription health.

Technical Architecture

Uses the Flutter development framework, ensuring a seamless cross-platform experience. Backend uses Firebase for real-time data processing. Integrating Google Search\Map, ChatGPT’s API for intelligent statistics and personalized recommendations.
